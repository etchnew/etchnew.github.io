About EtchNew
Conjured using the mastery of light, the Wizards of International School Of Photonics with a clear sight for the future to create a youth with a deep understanding of fundamental, we bring you EtchNew2020.

Everything, from the distant stars to the fireflies at night, signifies the mastery of light. Set in the backdrop of various disciplines of physical science, the annual optics fest EtchNew2020 brings together both the fundamental and advanced concepts and applications of photonics. Join us in the journey to embark on the advent, development, and future of light.
